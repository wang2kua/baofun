# Sleep sounds — sources & licenses

Played in sleep mode (white-noise content), looping at the set volume, fading
out over the last 30 seconds of a 30-minute window.

| File | Sound | License | Source |
|------|-------|---------|--------|
| `01_brown_noise.ogg` | Brown noise (60s loop) | CC0 | Wikimedia Commons — "Brown noise 15-00 69kbps.mp3", trimmed to 60s mono OGG |

Brown noise is the deepest/gentlest of the noise colors (softer than white or
pink), well suited to sleep. This is a real bundled CC0 recording, not
synthesized. Additional gentle-song content for sleep mode reuses the bundled
贝乐虎 songs.
